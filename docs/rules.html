Important guidelines to ensure a positive environment.

General Conduct

All users are expected to behave respectfully towards others, fostering a welcoming, friendly, and inclusive environment. Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.

Content Guidelines

Appropriate Content:

All content shared must be suitable for users of all ages. Offensive, hateful, or explicit material is strictly prohibited. Content must adhere to our family-friendly standards to maintain a safe and respectful space for everyone.

Attribution:

When sharing others' work, always give proper credit to the original creator. Respecting intellectual property is a fundamental part of fostering a supportive community.

Character Images:

Character images should be appropriate for all audiences. Any content deemed inappropriate, including explicit or suggestive material, will be removed to ensure a positive and respectful environment for all users.

Important: Character images must not depict real people. Only fictional characters are allowed to ensure privacy and respect for individuals.

Posting Guidelines:

When posting content, please keep the community's guidelines in mind. If your content may include themes that some users might find sensitive, it is helpful to provide context or a warning to ensure a respectful environment for all members.

Prohibited Activities

The following activities are not allowed:

  • Spamming or excessive self-promotion
  • Sharing personal information without consent
  • Using multiple accounts to evade restrictions
  • Creating characters that deceptively resemble real people
  • Creating or using characters that promote harm, violence, or explicit content
  • Engaging in any inappropriate behavior towards minors on the platform

Enforcement

Violations of these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community, depending on the severity of the action. We reserve the right to take necessary action to maintain a safe and family-friendly environment for all users.

Reporting Issues

If you notice any violations of these rules, please report them to the moderators at report@botbridge.net.
